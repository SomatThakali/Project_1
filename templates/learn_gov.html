GOVERNORS ISLAND NIGHTS

A quick ferry ride from downtown Manhattan, this peaceful oasis is nestled near the hills of historic Governors Island. You’ll be surrounded by sprawling green space with unparalleled views of the Statue of Liberty across the New York Harbor.
It’s a retreat unlike any other in the world.

GOVERNORS DINING EXPERIENCE

DINNER

For a traditional restaurant experience visit our restaurant for a sit down dining experience. Our á la carte menu always features a mix of healthy and savory entrees followed by sweet summertime desserts.

Menus change based on seasonal availability to ensure we provide only the freshest and locally sourced produce.

For something more informal, we can set you up with everything you need for a harbor side BBQ, including beverages, table settings, and ingredients for the main course and sides fully ready to grill. The best part is we will take care of the clean up.

HOW TO GET THERE

Collective Governors Island is located eight minutes from Lower Manhattan and five minutes from Brooklyn Bridge Park via ferry. We’re easily accessible via public transportation. There’s no need to rent a car as Governors Island is a car-free environment and you can explore the island by foot or via bicycle. Local airports include Newark International Airport, LaGuardia Airport and JFK International Airport. From there, you can take public transportation or a taxi to the 10 South Street Ferry Terminal or Pier 6 at Brooklyn Bridge Park. From there, take the Governors Island Ferry.
